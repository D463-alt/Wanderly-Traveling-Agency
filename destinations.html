Before You Go

Travel Tips

Check Passport Validity

Most countries require 6 months of validity beyond your travel dates.

Carry Some Local Cash

Cards aren't accepted everywhere — keep small bills for markets and taxis.

Pack For The Season

Check the local forecast a week out — weather shifts fast in the mountains.

Get Travel Insurance

Covers cancellations, delays, and unexpected medical costs abroad.

Learn A Few Phrases

"Hello," "thank you," and "how much" go a long way anywhere.

Back Up Your Photos

Cloud-sync daily so a lost phone doesn't mean lost memories.
