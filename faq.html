Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you book.

How do I book a trip?
Browse a destination or package, then hit "Book Now" or fill out the contact form. A travel consultant will confirm details and send a secure payment link within 24 hours.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, cancellations are accepted up to 14 days before departure for a full refund minus a small processing fee. Later cancellations follow our tiered refund policy.
Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are issued based on how far in advance you cancel, outlined in our Refund Policy. Weather-related and airline cancellations are handled case by case.
Are flights included?
Most packages cover accommodation, transport, meals, and activities, but not international flights. We're happy to help you find and book flights separately.
Do I need travel insurance?
We strongly recommend it. Insurance covers trip cancellations, delays, lost luggage, and medical emergencies abroad — and some destinations require proof of coverage.
Can I customize my package?
Absolutely. Every package can be adjusted — swap hotels, add extra days, change activities, or build a fully custom itinerary from scratch.
Do you provide visa assistance?
Yes, our team provides visa requirement checklists and documentation support for most destinations as part of your booking.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and popular digital wallets. Payment plans are available on select packages.
Are children allowed?
Yes, many of our packages are family-friendly, and we offer dedicated family itineraries with age-appropriate activities.
Do you offer group discounts?
Groups of 6 or more qualify for discounted rates on most packages. Contact our team for a custom group quote.
Is customer support available 24/7?
Yes, our support desk is staffed around the clock so help is available no matter what time zone you're traveling in.
Can I change my booking date?
Date changes are free up to 30 days before departure, subject to availability. Closer to departure, a small rescheduling fee may apply.
What documents are required?
A valid passport is required for all international trips, along with any visas or vaccination certificates specific to your destination.
How early should I book?
We recommend booking 2-3 months ahead for the best rates and availability, especially during peak season destinations.
Are meals included?
Meal inclusions vary by package — check each package's details page for exactly what's covered, from breakfast-only to all-inclusive.
