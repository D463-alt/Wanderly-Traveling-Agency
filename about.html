Our Story

A Decade Of Getting People Where They Belong

Wanderly started in 2011 as a two-person desk booking weekend beach trips. Today we're a team of 40 travel specialists, guides, and planners spread across 12 countries — but the job hasn't changed: get you somewhere unforgettable, without the stress of getting there.

Our mission is to provide affordable, safe, and memorable travel experiences while delivering outstanding customer service. Our vision is to become one of the world's most trusted travel agencies by connecting people with incredible destinations — one thoughtfully planned trip at a time.
